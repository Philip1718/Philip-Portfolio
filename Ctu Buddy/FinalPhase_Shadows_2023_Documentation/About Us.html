The story of CTU-Buddy

Our purpose

The purpose of the CTU Buddy is to provide students with a professional and straightforward user-friendly platform. It serves as an invaluable tool in fostering academic success and facilitating an enhanced learning environment at CTU. The platform aims to assist students in efficiently managing their timetables, actively engaging in discussions, easily sharing resources, and effectively connecting with their peers and educators. Overall, the CTU Buddy aims to create a seamless and efficient platform that empowers students to excel in their academic journey while promoting productive collaboration and communication within the CTU community.

Our vision

The vision of the CTU-Buddy project is to become the go-to educational system for students at City Technical University, providing them with a state-of-the-art platform that transforms the way they learn and interact with course materials and their peers. The project envisions a future where CTU-Buddy is recognized for its innovative features, user-friendly interface, and seamless functionality across different devices. The vision is to create a digital learning environment that empowers students, enhances their academic experience, and fosters collaboration and engagement in their educational journey.

Our mission

The mission of the CTU-Buddy project is to create a comprehensive and interactive educational system that enhances the learning experience for students at City Technical University (CTU). It aims to provide students with a user-friendly platform to manage their timetables, engage in collaborative discussions, share educational resources, and connect with their peers and educators effectively. The project's mission is to revolutionize the educational experience at CTU by fostering academic success and facilitating an enhanced learning environment.
